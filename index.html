Course Curriculum

11 Modules. Zero Fluff.

Every module is designed to move you forward. You'll go from understanding how private label works all the way to building your brand, running ads, and scaling your revenue.

01
Foundation

Setting Up Your Business

Get your foundation right from day one. We walk through creating your Amazon Seller Central account and setting up your legal business structure. An LLC if you're in the US, or a Corp if you're in Canada.

02
Research

Product Research

Learn how to use product research tools to find profitable niches with real demand and manageable competition. We also cover how to analyze negative reviews to identify differentiation opportunities your competitors are missing.

03
Sourcing

Sourcing Your Product

Find reliable manufacturers on Alibaba and beyond. Learn how to request samples, compare quality, and negotiate pricing, MOQ (Minimum Order Quantity), and delivery terms so you get the best deal on your first order.

04
Branding

Branding & Packaging

Build a brand that stands out. We cover logo design, packaging strategy, sustainability considerations, and how to craft a brand story that connects with customers and sets you apart from generic competitors.

05
Listing

Product Listing Optimization

Write keyword-rich titles, compelling bullet points, and detailed descriptions that convert. Learn Amazon SEO fundamentals and how to brief a photographer for product images that convert.

06
Reviews

Getting Your First Reviews

Understand how Amazon's Early Reviewer Program and Vine work, and how to ethically build your review base early. We cover what's allowed, what gets accounts banned, and the right way to generate social proof from day one.

07
Launch

Launch Strategy

Execute a launch that gets traction fast. Learn how to run Amazon PPC campaigns from scratch, structure limited-time promotions, and use early advertising spend to climb the rankings and build organic momentum.

08
Fulfillment

Fulfillment by Amazon (FBA)

Understand exactly how FBA works and how to prep your products to Amazon's guidelines. Learn how to ship inventory to fulfillment centers (your supplier can handle this), and let Amazon take care of storage, packing, and delivery.

09
Marketing

Ongoing Marketing & Promotion

Scale beyond your launch. We cover email marketing to gather reviews and cross-sell products, Amazon Sponsored Ads strategy, and how to tap into external channels like social media and influencers to drive traffic to your listing.

10
Growth

Monitor & Adapt

Turn your launch into a long-term business. Learn how to analyze customer reviews for product improvements, adjust pricing based on demand and competition, and continuously optimize your PPC campaigns based on real performance data.

11
Scaling

Stand Out & Scale

Build a brand that competitors can't easily copy. We cover crafting a strong Unique Value Proposition, focusing on quality over quantity, diversifying your marketing channels beyond Amazon, and educating your buyers to create loyal long-term customers.
